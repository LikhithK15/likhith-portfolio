About

I write things

I'm a freelance content writer and scriptwriter based in India. I specialise in research-driven storytelling, the kind that takes a dense, complicated topic and makes it feel urgent and human.

My work spans YouTube documentary scripts, short-form reel content, investigative journalism, travel writing, and personal essays. I've covered geopolitics, banking fraud, internet mysteries, energy crises, and the places in between.

If you need someone who can find the story inside a government report, a financial scandal, or a travel destination, that's what I do.
